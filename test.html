Building the future of African mobility

Our motorcycle

We assemble and sell electric motorcycles, based on a PayGo (rent-to-own) model: drivers pay weekly and own their motorcycle after 2 years. Our technology limits the use of the motorcycle to paying clients only. This allows us to secure the repayment.

Included in the contract :

  • Insurance
  • Helmet
  • Maintenance

Our battery swapping service

Zembo remains the battery owner. We operate a network of hybrid (solar and grid power, at 90% hydro in Uganda) recharge stations where batteries are charged. As soon as their battery is empty, drivers swap their empty battery against a recharged one. They pay for the energy used. Our 100% solar recharge stations allow us to charge the batteries even in off-grid areas and during grid blackout.
